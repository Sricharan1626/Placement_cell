Our Mission

At Symbiosis Institute of Technology, Hyderabad, the Training and Placement (T&P) Cell serves as the cornerstone for enabling students to transition successfully from academia to industry. Guided by the values of excellence, innovation, and holistic development, the Cell strives to empower students to become future-ready professionals equipped with technical expertise, professional confidence, and industry-aligned competencies.

Key Objectives

To organize campus recruitment with top companies, prepare students via aptitude & coding assessments, and enhance personality and communication skills.

Our Core Purpose

Mission: To empower students into becoming industry-ready professionals through a dynamic, inclusive, and future-forward talent transformation ecosystem.

  • To organize campus recruitment drives for B.Tech students by inviting reputed companies.
  • To prepare students for placements through aptitude and coding assessments, and interview skills training.
  • To build and sustain regular engagement with the industry through seminars, guest lectures, and collaborations.

Policy Highlights

  • A student may apply to any number of companies but will be excluded from further processes after receiving two job offers.
  • A "Dream Offer" (compensation of INR 10 LPA or above) is a separate opportunity.
  • Eligibility requires a minimum academic score of 60% throughout all stages.
  • All communication between companies and students must be routed exclusively through the T&P Cell.

Participation Guidelines

Placement Disclaimer: The Institute facilitates opportunities but does not guarantee placement. Final selection is at the sole discretion of the recruiting company.

  • Eligibility: Minimum 60% academic score across all stages is mandatory.
  • Withdrawal: Students may withdraw after a Pre-Placement Talk. However, once they participate in selection rounds, accepting an offer makes them ineligible for future drives.
  • Offer Policy: A student can receive up to two regular offers.
